The Ideator

Info
This is an interface to enable quick iterations of ideation. You get a prompt for how to move with your idea and can start writing; once you run out, click Next (or Ctrl + Enter) to get a new prompt. When you finish this chain of ideation, click Finish & Copy (or Ctrl + Shift + Enter) to copy your chain. To download the chain, click Download. To change the list of prompts, scroll to the bottom of this page. Your writing is saved locally in your browser. No one else has access to it.
